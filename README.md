Governance-first, AI-assisted simulation research exploring systemic risk, escalation dynamics, and restraint-based decision analysis across complex domains.


Author: Yasir Farooq

AI Assistance: ChatGPT (GPT-5-mini) used as an assistive reasoning platform

---

Executive Overview

This repository documents a governance-first, AI-assisted simulation research lineage focused on understanding systemic risk, escalation dynamics, and institutional decision failure in complex environments. The work prioritizes how decisions break down over predicting outcomes.

Explicit constraints:

Not predictive

Not deployable

Not operational software

Exploratory and research-grade only


The lineage exists to stress-test assumptions, narratives, and governance logic under uncertainty—not to offer real-world prescriptions.


---

Design Philosophy

The design emphasizes restraint over capability.

Key principles:

Governance precedes scale and power

Assumptions are stress-tested, not optimized

Silence, delay, and non-action are treated as analytical variables

Version freezing and rollback are features, not failures


The objective is to preserve legitimacy under complexity, not to maximize output.
